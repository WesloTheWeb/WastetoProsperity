Our Goals

Waste To Prosperity/Utterly Amazing

  • We here at Waste To Prosperity are committed to making everyone aware of the fact that millions of gallons of wasted milk could be used and potentially prevent hunger. By connecting people with useful resources, our goal is to bring farmers that have excess milk, together with public schools and other communities, who can use that milk to feed hungry children or others in need. It is an Utterly Amazing solution.

Why we are passionate about Waste to Prosperity

  • There has been a growing crisis of farmers being forced to waste approximately 43 million gallons of milk due to a saturated market and unavailable buyers. Part of our mission is raise awareness of this issue, and perhaps partner up with different organizations to direct the excess milk to communities in need. Our long term goal is sustainability and to increase the relationship between the farmer and city communities.
  • Our target audiences are farmers and communities in need. Wisconsin has a lot of dairy farms that typically produce milk. Their normal consumer was the Canadian market, but recently, Canada created a policy that undercuts the sale of dairy products from Wisconsin, and Canada stated that, “They are not responsible or obligated to buy dairy products from the U.S. when they have their own dairy farms”. There are many schools and communities that are underfunded and can benefit from the excess amount of milk. It is important to note that milk can also be used as a reagent for other products such as cheese, or other consumables.

  • We are aiming for a viable solution to this problem. We plan on partnering up with other farmer associations and organizations to extend our level of involvement and hope that with enough potential donors, our goals can be realized.
